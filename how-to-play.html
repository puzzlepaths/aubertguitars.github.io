How To Play

There's been a mysterious murder in the city and the Bath Police Department needs your help getting to the bottom of it. Finding the clues will take you all around the city of Bath - but can you solve the puzzle? Get ready to unlock your inner Sherlock Holmes!

1. Purchase Your Game

Simply click here to buy your game. After paying online, you'll instantly receive the downloadable game pack. It will also be emailed to you for safekeeping so you always have a copy of it.

2. Print it Off

Whenever you're ready, print the game off and you'll be ready to go. You can also view it on a tablet device. We advise against using a smartphone as the screen size will make things awkward. It's recommended to print it off as it's easier to make notes and share amongst a group!

3. Head to the Starting Point

You can start solving the puzzle whenever you're ready. So long as there's daylight, there are no restrictions! The game lasts 2-4 hours so it's great for both a morning or afternoon activity. The starting point is none other than Bath Abbey, right in the middle of the city.

4. Start Solving!

All you need are suitable clothes and shoes for walking around town. Don't worry - the route will be along paths and won't require you to walk in muddy terrain. Grab your case files, a pen, put on your thinking cap and you're all set!
