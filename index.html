What's A Puzzle Path?

Puzzle Paths is an outdoor murder mystery game set in the city of Bath. Your case file will present you with a list of suspects and directions to clues scattered across the city. It's up to you to solve the clues, uncover the killer and crack the case! Along the way, you'll see the best of Bath as well as some hidden gems. You'll visit the Abbey, the Royal Crescent, the famous Weir and much more.

Whether you're new to Bath or looking for a fun activity for the weekend, Puzzle Paths is something different to explore the city in a fun, fresh way!

Great for families, couples and groups of friends.
Suitable for ages 6+

Flexible Times

Start the game whenever you want and solve the clues at your own speed. It's a self-paced game so you're in control. Take breaks, enjoy a quick detour or solve it as fast as possible - whatever you prefer!

Explore Bath

You'll see the best sights in Bath as you complete your Puzzle Path. It's a fun, interactive way to see the city. Solving the mystery will take you to new and exciting places!

Value For Money

You only need to buy one game per group. Simply share the case files between you as you solve it together. Have a great day out with your friends, family or partner without breaking the bank!
